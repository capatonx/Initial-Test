### Stop 1: Waiheke Island
- **Dates:** November 8–10, 2026 (2 nights)
- **Accommodation:** Home on Waiheke
- **Transfer in:** SkyBus from airport → Fullers360 ferry from Pier 1 (~35 min)
- **Transfer out (Nov 10):** Fullers360 back to Auckland (~9–10 am), pick up rental car in Auckland CBD, drive to Raglan (~2 hrs)
- **Island transport:** Waiheke Bus Company, taxis, e-bike hire at Matiatia Wharf — no rental car needed on island
- **Highlights:** Wine touring (Tantalus, Mudbrick, Casita Miro), The Oyster Inn, Waiheke harbour

### Stop 2: Raglan
- **Dates:** November 10–12, 2026 (2 nights)
- **Accommodation:** Home in Raglan (TBC — not yet booked)
- **Transfer:** Ferry back to Auckland, pick up rental car (Auckland CBD), drive ~2 hrs via SH1/SH23
- **Car hire note:** Automatic transmission recommended (driving on left for first time); Go Rentals, Budget, or Avis
- **Highlights:** Surfing, Bridal Veil Falls, harbour walks
- **Departure (Nov 12):** Drive Raglan → Hobbiton (~1.5 hrs, depart ~7:30 am for 9:30 am tour)

### Stop 3: Rotorua
- **Dates:** November 12–13, 2026 (1 night)
- **Accommodation:** Regent of Rotorua Boutique Hotel (Hinemaru Street, central Rotorua)
- **Day logistics:** Raglan → Hobbiton (~1.5 hrs) → Kaituna rafting (~1 hr) → Regent of Rotorua (~20 min). Arrive ~5:30 pm.
- **Hobbiton:** Book in advance at hobbitontours.com — 9:30 am tour start, November peak season
- **Kaituna rafting:** Kaituna Cascades or K2K Rafting — 7 m Tutea Falls, ~2 hr session, depart Hobbiton ~12:30 pm
- **Transfer out:** Drive to ROT airport (~20 min), return car at airport, fly ROT → AKL → ZQN

### Stop 4: Queenstown
- **Dates:** November 13–18, 2026 (5 nights)
- **Accommodation:** QT Queenstown Hotel (Stanley Street)
- **Transfer in:** Flight ROT → AKL → ZQN; Uber or taxi from ZQN (~10 min)
- **Activities & logistics:**
  - Ben Lomond hike (Nov 14): Skyline Gondola (~NZD 32) + 4 hr return summit trail, start by 8 am
  - Mt Cook heli hike (Nov 15): Over The Top Helicopters (OTT) or Heliworks — weather-dependent, book well ahead
  - E-bike wine tour (Nov 16): Around The Basin or Queenstown Wine Trail — Gibbston Valley, full day, guide included
  - Roy's Peak (Nov 17): Drive to Wanaka (~45 min), 16 km return ~5 hrs, start 7 am
- **Highlights:** Ben Lomond hike, Mt Cook heli hike, e-bike wine tour, Roy's Peak

### Stop 5: Fiordland (Manapouri)
- **Dates:** November 18–21, 2026 (3 nights)
- **Accommodation:** Cabot Lodge
- **Transfer:** Drive from Queenstown (~2.5 hrs)
- **Highlights:** Milford Sound day cruise, fly fishing
- **Departure (Nov 21):** Drive to Queenstown Airport (ZQN), fly ZQN → AKL → NAN

### Stop 6: Fiji (Momi Bay)
- **Dates:** November 21–26, 2026 (5 nights)
- **Accommodation:** Marriott Resort Momi Bay (~45 min south of Nadi Airport)
- **Transfer in:** Flight ZQN → AKL → NAN; resort transfer from Nadi Airport
- **Highlights:** Scuba diving, fishing charter, surfing, snorkeling, Port Denarau marina dining (Indigo, Nadina, Rhum-Ba)
- **Return:** November 26, NAN → LAX → JFK/EWR
